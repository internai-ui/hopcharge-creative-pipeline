# Sara - Hopcharge Customer Character

Sara is Hopcharge's recurring customer character. She appears in all ad creatives wherever a customer is depicted. Always reference this description to ensure visual consistency across every image and video generated.

---

## Physical Description

**Age:** Mid-to-late 20s (approximately 26-28)
**Ethnicity:** North Indian woman
**Skin tone:** Warm medium-brown (wheatish/golden undertone) - classic North Indian complexion, not too dark, not too fair, with a natural healthy glow

### Face
- Round, soft face structure with full cheeks and a gentle jawline
- Large, almond-shaped dark brown/near-black eyes - expressive and lively
- Naturally arched, well-defined dark eyebrows
- Soft broad nose, delicate features overall
- Full lips with a natural warm smile - genuine, often showing teeth
- Minimal to warm-toned makeup (nude or peachy lip, soft eyeliner) - polished but never heavily made up
- Small hoop earrings or simple studs in most settings

### Hair (most distinctive feature - critical for consistency)
- Extremely long, thick, and voluminous dark hair (near-black to dark brown)
- Naturally wavy / softly curled - waves begin from roughly mid-length and cascade downward in loose, flowing waves
- Falls well below the shoulder, typically to mid-back or lower back
- Almost always worn down and loose (use a high ponytail only for explicitly athletic/gym scenes)
- The thickness and length are her single most recognizable trait - always render it as lush, healthy, and full-bodied. Never thin, flat, or short.

### Build
- Medium height (~165 cm / 5'5")
- Athletic and visibly toned - especially arms and midriff
- Healthy feminine curves, confident upright posture
- She carries herself with ease and self-assurance

---

## Style Reference

Sara dresses for an urban, aspirational, modern Indian lifestyle:

| Context | What she wears |
|---|---|
| Casual Western | Neutral linen/cotton button-down (beige, white) + dark slim jeans; colorful printed tees |
| Indian ethnic | Blue floral printed kurtas; Indian-print cotton dresses with colored border trim |
| Professional/formal | Fitted bodycon dresses in dark navy or sparkle fabric |
| Athletic | Coordinated workout sets (dark teal, black, bold solid colors) - crop top + high-waist leggings |
| Modern fusion | Two-piece denim coordinates; contemporary indo-western silhouettes |

---

## Personality

- Confident and self-possessed - comfortable in any setting
- Warm and approachable - her natural expression is a soft or full smile
- Active urban lifestyle: gym, coffee shops, work, outdoor activities, social outings
- Modern, independent professional woman who makes her own decisions
- Aspirational but relatable - not intimidating, genuinely likeable

## Usage Rules

1. **Every customer in every ad is Sara** - replace any generic "Indian urban professional" character description with the strings above.
2. **Hair is non-negotiable** - if the hair reads as short, thin, or straight, the character is wrong. Always specify "very long thick dark wavy hair."
3. **She is the customer, not the technician** - the Hopcharge van operator / technician can be any appropriate background character. Sara is the one who owns the EV and is being served.
4. **Outfit varies with context** - pick the appropriate style from the table above. The physical description (face, hair, build, skin) must stay constant.
5. **Do not add a name or dialogue** to the character in image prompts - she is a visual anchor, not a named actor.
